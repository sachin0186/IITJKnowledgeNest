About Us

Welcome to our open-source platform dedicated to providing high-quality study materials for students at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Jodhpur. At the heart of our mission is the belief that knowledge should be freely accessible to all, fostering an environment where learning is a shared experience.

Our Commitment to IIT Jodhpur Students

IIT Jodhpur stands as a beacon of excellence in technical education in India. Recognizing the unique challenges and academic rigor that students face at this esteemed institution, we have curated a comprehensive collection of study materials spanning various disciplines.

Why Open Source?

Education is a powerful tool for empowerment, and we believe in making it accessible to everyone. By adopting an open-source model, we ensure that students have free and unrestricted access to a wealth of study materials, textbooks, lecture notes, and more.
